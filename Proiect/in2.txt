Ingredients.
water = 500 ml,
potatoes = 1 kg,
salt = 5 g,
pepper = 5 g,

Method.

Put onion into the mixing bowl.
Put corn into the mixing bowl.
Fold corn into the mixing bowl.

Take meat from refrigerator.
