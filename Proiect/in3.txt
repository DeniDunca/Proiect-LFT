Ingredients.
water = 500 ml,
sugar = 300 g,
milk = 100 ml,
honey = 50 g,
chocolate = 200 g,
apple = 2 pieces 

Method.
Put water into the mixing bowl.
Put sugar into the mixing bowl.
Put milk into the mixing bowl.
Put honey into the mixing bowl.
Put chocolate into the mixing bowl.
Combine 1 piece apple into the mixing bowl.
Divide 10 ml milk into the mixing bowl.
Refrigerate for 3 hours.
Baking dishes.
Clean mixing bowl.
